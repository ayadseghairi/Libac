## آفاق العمل بعد التخرج

- The paths ahead of you? Wide open.
- Academic & Research Path
- Finish your Master 2, and the doors to a doctorate swing open — in Algeria or abroad, working within the LMD system to push the boundaries of robotics and autonomous systems research. And once you earn that doctorate? You don't just join the academic world — you lead it. As a Researcher-Professor, you'll lecture, mentor, and train the next generation of engineers, all while driving innovation inside university research labs.
- Professional & Industrial Path
- Or take the industry route — and it's a powerful one. Major national players like Sonatrach need engineers who can design and maintain intelligent automation systems for critical industrial and energy operations. Global tech giants like Samsung and Innova are hiring embedded systems engineers and robotics developers. Want something bigger? Sign advanced R&D contracts with the National Army, working inside cutting-edge defense and logistics labs. And if entrepreneurship is calling your name — the school's business incubator has your back: seed funding, free office space, legal and administrative mentorship, and full tax and customs exemptions to turn your idea into a real business.
